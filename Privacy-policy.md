# Privacy Policy for Essa by Vanessa and McClain

Essa by Vanessa and McClain respects your privacy. This application is designed to allow users to explore fashion designs fabrics and garment visualization tools.

Information Collection
The app may collect limited information required to operate features of the application such as device information and optional user provided measurements for garment fitting.

Use of Information
Any information collected is used solely to operate and improve the functionality of the application and to provide personalized garment visualization and design features.

Data Sharing
Essa by Vanessa and McClain does not sell rent or distribute personal information to third parties.

Security
Reasonable measures are taken to protect user information and ensure the integrity of the platform.

Third Party Services
Some features of the app may rely on third party services or libraries which may collect limited technical data required for functionality.

Changes to This Policy
This privacy policy may be updated periodically as the application evolves.

Contact
For questions regarding this privacy policy please contact the repository owner through GitHub.
